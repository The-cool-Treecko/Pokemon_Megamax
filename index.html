Features


New Region



In the new region of Aurres a darker-than-usual plot takes place. Will you survive, or perish?

Story Based



The game is more story based than battle based.

Mutant Pokemon



Genetically engineered stronger-than-usual pokemon made by Team Saber for world domination. You can't get past these easily.

Harder Difficulty



Difficulty is increased to make the game more challenging, its easier to get the "Don't do a Nuzlocke" achievement now.




  And many more features, such as:


 > Achievements System. More achievements will be added every update.

 > Pokemon up to gen 8. Don't forget the fakemons though.

 > Music from different artists like GlitchxCity,Zame and Luigigigas. Some official and custom music present too.

 > Gen 5 styled GUI. Includes some custom GUI too.

 > Game overs. If you lose battles during certain parts of the game, you get a game over.

 > Level caps. Level caps are increased after every gym.

 > Gen 4 styled Overworld graphics and Gen 5 styled Maps.

 > Sidequests. More sidequests will be added every chapter.

 > Unreal time system. Every one second a minute passes in the game.

 > And much more...


Story elements


Team Saber

Team saber is an anarchorous group of criminals who use pokemon for evil.

Necrozma

A legendary that saved the protagonist from drowning when the ship crashed. He seems quite nice though other Legendary Pokemon consider Necrozma rather eccentric.

Dark Storyline

Some parts of this game are not meant for kids, they contain violence, bloodshed, deaths on screen. If you are disturbed by this then don't play the game (or just cover your eyes during those parts lol).
